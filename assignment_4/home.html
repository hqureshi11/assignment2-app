Who We Are

It all started in a small kitchen in Squirrel Hill in 1984 - Van Halen's "Jump" was blasting off the turntable, test tubes littered the counter, and the room smelled of freshly-baked goodness. Betty Bunbun, a chemistry student at Carnegie Mellon University, spent all summer exploring hundreds of ingredient combinations to concont the perfect cinnamon bun. After decades of love and tender baking, Bun Bun Bake Shop has grown to become one of the premiere bakeries in Western Pennsylvania. And now, you can have all your favorites delivered right to your doorstep!
